Terms as of 2025:

* **Summary**:  
  * As with all groups in the Islandora Community, members of this Committee and attendees of the Metadata Interest Meetings must agree to abide by the [Code of Conduct](https://www.islandora.ca/code-of-conduct)  
  * Further information about this group, including current membership, is available on the [islandora-community wiki](https://github.com/Islandora/islandora-community/wiki/Technical-Advisory-Group-%28TAG%29) and the Documentation Committee repository  
  * Members and attendees are encouraged to  fill out the [Onboarding Form](https://forms.gle/BKbwvHELJmMc9MJu6) to be added to the Islandora community  communication channels   
    * Communication Channels   
        1. Slack channel  
        2. Documentation Committee Github page  
* **Purpose and scope**:  
  * The purpose of the Islandora Documentation Committee is to coordinate the creation and maintenance of documentation by the Islandora community, and to support the Islandora Leadership Group to make decisions on best practices as it relates to the development of the Islandora Framework  
  * Specific goals, activities, and outcomes include:  
    * Establish best practices for Islandora documentation.   
    * Facilitate and encourage a community of practice for documentation within the larger Islandora community  
    * Establish collaborative workflows for creating and maintaining documentation, working with other committees and groups as necessary  
    * Assist Islandora users and contributors who have questions about the documentation  
    * Review and discuss documentation-related issues reported in Islandora issue tracking systems  
    * Provide stable documentation endpoints/’hooks’ for integration with the Islandora UI and coordinate improved implementation of documentation directly in the UI  
    * Identify and prioritize documentation gaps and issues based on community feedback  
* **Membership**  
  * Anyone in the Islandora community may join the Committee  
  * A list of current DC co-conveners will be maintained at the DC Committee's Github Repository  
* **Roles and Responsibilities**  
  * The Committee must identify one member to serve as a representative to the Leadership Group, who will attend those meetings on a regular basis (at least once a month) and provide updates as to the activities of the Committee, for a period of at least one year starting in July  
  * The Committee may have as many Convenors as they see fit  
    * A call for agenda items will be posted at least 1 week prior to the meeting. The convener(s) will appoint a note taker for the meeting and meeting notes will be made available to the community via the community wiki.  
  * The Committee should meet at least once a month  
  * Convenor(s) will call meetings when needed and share meeting agendas where appropriate.  
  * Meetings are held on Zoom. To join a meeting click the link in the meeting agenda ([https://github.com/islandora-interest-groups/Islandora-Documentation-Committee/tree/main/meetings](https://github.com/islandora-interest-groups/Islandora-Documentation-Committee/tree/main/meetings))  
  * A convenor will appoint a note taker for the meeting and meeting notes will be made available online after the meeting  
* **Communications**  
  * When communication with the community is necessary we will use the community mailing list and documentation Slack channel.
